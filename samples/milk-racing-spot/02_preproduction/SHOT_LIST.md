# Shot List — Fuel the Fastest

Three hero frames, 16:9 widescreen, generated via Gemini Nano Banana Pro.
Each shot ships as a standalone PNG usable as either a print ad, thumbnail, or first-frame for a Veo 3.1 motion extension later (not executed in this sample).

---

## Shot 01 — Approach (pit stop hand-off)

**Role in arc:** Setup. Viewer recognizes the motorsport world, then the incongruity of the milk bottle pulls them in.

**Composition:**
- Wide-angle, three-quarter rear view of a sleek modern formula-style race car braking into pit lane at golden hour
- Tire smoke swirling around the rear wing
- Pit crew in matching warm-charcoal and cream racing suits moving in choreographed motion
- Foreground subject (sharp focus): one crew member kneeling, holding up a tall frosted bottle of milk with a stainless-steel racing straw, offering it toward the cockpit
- Driver visible behind the visor, just lifting it, small confident smile
- Warm stadium lights catching the car's body panels, slight anamorphic lens flare
- Background: blurred garage bay with warm ambient tones

**Camera / style:** Cinematic commercial photography, Canon EOS R5 + 50mm f/1.4 aesthetic, shallow depth of field, golden-hour warmth, slight motion blur on crew hands only.

**Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (1536×1024 or larger)
**Provider:** Gemini Nano Banana Pro (photoreal, non-text)

## Shot 02 — The Hero Pour (product beauty)

**Role in arc:** Hero moment. The product carries the campaign — milk as performance fuel.

**Composition:**
- Extreme close-up macro shot
- Ice-cold milk pouring in slow motion from a brushed-chrome pitcher into a matte-black stainless sports bottle
- Droplets suspended mid-air, a crown splash forming at the bottle's lip
- Condensation beads on the bottle exterior
- Background: blurred warm heat-haze of a pit lane, amber and cream tones, no legible shapes
- Subject centered, razor-sharp focus on the pour itself; everything else melts to bokeh

**Camera / style:** Studio-grade commercial product photography. Phase One IQ4 / Hasselblad beauty-shot aesthetic. Extremely shallow depth of field. Advertising-gloss, not hyperreal — warm and inviting, not clinical.

**Aspect ratio:** 16:9
**Provider:** Gemini Nano Banana Pro (product photography tier)

## Shot 03 — Victory (podium spray)

**Role in arc:** Payoff. Campaign signature — the champagne-spray moment reimagined as a milk spray.

**Composition:**
- Low-angle cinematic shot, driver centered on a podium platform
- Full race suit, helmet just removed and held loosely at the hip, hair slightly tousled, triumphant grin
- Trophy visible on a stand beside them
- Driver is shaking a large bottle that's shooting a cascading arc of pure white liquid (milk) into the air
- Droplets catching warm stadium lighting like champagne spray, suspended in midair
- Background: cheering crowd silhouetted, confetti drifting down, bright stadium spotlights bleeding into lens flare
- Composition leaves clean space at top-right for optional campaign lockup in finishing

**Camera / style:** Cinematic sports commercial, slight slow-motion feel, dramatic dynamic range, warm spotlight on driver + cooler rim lights.

**Aspect ratio:** 16:9
**Provider:** Gemini Nano Banana Pro (cinematic)
